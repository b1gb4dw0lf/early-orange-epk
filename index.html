Bio

At the end of 2020, Vladimir Popu and Kaan Kara found themselves as the remnants of a dissolved band looking for a new one. Around the same time, Saad El Adraoui and Petar Marčeta met and decided to search for a band. The mutual search connected the four via online forums during covid restrictions in Amsterdam and with the help of a random name generator, the band formed.

Drawing inspiration from a diverse range of artists and genres, including garage rock, neo-psychedelic rock, and dance-punk, Early Orange has developed a unique and energetic sound. The band's latest singles, "X", "The Tower", and "Don't Look Left" showcase their signature blend of styles.

Currently, Early Orange is hard at work on their next single "Radio". In the meantime, you can catch them performing in and around Amsterdam. Keep an eye out for updates on show dates and new music releases by following the band on Spotify/Instagram/Bandcamp.

Thank you for considering Early Orange for your event or publication. We hope to have the opportunity to share our music with you soon.
